Croissant club sandwhich

croissant club sandwhich

a croissant turned into a club sandwhich with choice of turkey or ham with cheese , lettuce, tomato, and mayo

Ingredients

  • croissant
  • turkey/Ham
  • Cheese
  • lettuce
  • tomato
  • mayonaise

Steps

  1. grab croissant
  2. spread mayo on croissant
  3. put turkey
  4. add cheese
  5. add lettuce
  6. add tomato
  7. lightly pack sandwhich
  8. grab and eat
